The Four Tabs

🛒

Shopping List

Your active list for the shop. Add items manually or pull them in from Recipes and Staples. Tap any item to check it off as you go. Use Clear checked when you're done.
📦

Stock

Track what you have at home. Items are grouped as Cupboard, Fridge, or Freezer. Mark your essentials as Staples — things you always want in the house — so you can quickly spot what needs restocking and add it to your list with one tap.
📖

Recipes

Build recipes from your items. Each recipe shows which ingredients you already have in stock and which need buying. Hit Add missing to list to send only the gaps to your shopping list.
📅

Meal Planner

Drag recipes onto days of the week to plan your meals. Once your week is planned, add all the missing ingredients to your shopping list in one go.

⚠ Export to Save Your Data Permanently

The app stores everything in your browser's local storage — which means your recipes, items, and meal plans will be lost if you clear your browser data, switch devices, or reinstall the app.

Go to Settings (⚙) and tap Export regularly to download a backup file. You can restore everything at any time with Import. Do this before clearing your browser, switching phones, or sharing your recipes with someone else.

📱 Use It Like an App on Android

In Chrome on Android you can install the app to your home screen and use it fully offline:

  1. Open the app in Chrome.
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right) → "Add to Home screen".
  3. Tap Add — it appears on your home screen like a native app.
  4. For reliable offline access, also download the page: three-dot menu → "Download page". A downloaded file on your device is more dependable than browser cache, which can be cleared at any time.
