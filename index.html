This Site

Once upon a time if you wanted a space on the web you made yourself a website. When I was 12 I had a small corner of my own on the site that my dad had for a racehorse called Ice Cracker. It was great, anything was possible.

Since then the dark shadow of social media has cast over the web and we have been striaght jacketed into profiles and feeds.

Let a new light shine though! Building a personal website with complete creative control is now as close to free as it is ever going to get. And I'm here for it!
